About

Our story, our passion

Why are we doing this?

Why are we doing this? Our adventure with mobile applications began when we became parents. We wanted to give our children exactly what they needed. Therefore our first app was the Bee-Package game, which helps children in difficult moments and teaches them. Especially when for example, you drive a car. While developing our games, we noticed that not only children play them, but also adults willingly reach for puzzles and coloring books ... That is why we are looking for new solutions and technologies to provide you with interesting experiences. Our newest application is Dot Poly...

Who we are?

Why are we doing this? We are a group of Software Engineers with 10+ years of experience in the software industry. We have been involved in many multi-million $/EUR projects, including creating desktop application, embedded applications and internet based services. We have decided to expand our product portfolio and add mobile application development. Below page presents mobile applications created with love and passion just for you.
